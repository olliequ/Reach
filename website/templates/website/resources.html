We know that sometimes meditation and mood logging isn't enough - that's why we've decided to list external resources. They are listed below:




HEADSPACE

An Australian organisation that focuses on supporting young Australians’ mental health. Offers free and confidential therapy - online or in person - with qualified professionals, as well as career mentoring and general life advice.

BEYONDBLUE

An Australian non-for-profit organisation that supports people of all ages in treating anxiety, depression and suicidal thoughts. Offers confidential support with qualified therapists - online, via email, or over the phone.

LIFELINE

24 hour crisis support for Australians experiencing emotional distress, as well as advice for family and friends for effectively supporting others. Provides a safe space to discuss your needs, worries and concerns, in a safe, judgement-free environment.

MEETUP

A website to organise events, in-person or virtual, for people with similar interests. Find a new friendship group, sportsteam, or connect with people in your area who share your hobbies and interests.

HEADTOHEALTH

A government website that lists and aggregates digital resources to support the wellbeing and mental health of Australians. Whether you are trying to improve your own sense of wellbeing, looking for help with something that is bothering you, or helping someone you care about—Head to Health is a good place to start.
